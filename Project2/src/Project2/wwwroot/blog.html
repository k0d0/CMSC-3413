Galaxy Blaster!

2/19/17

Here are some images of the final project for my OOP class!


The requirements of the final were to create a game using the MVC design method, along with 3 or more design patterns


The game was a galaga like space shooter, with an incremental level system. Each level would be harder than the last (i.e More enemies would appear on the gamebaord the farther you progress)




Every 3rd level would be a "Boss" level. The boss had increase health and dropped "BombEnemies" which would hurt the player, He also spawned "bombEnemies" that dropped powerups for the player
